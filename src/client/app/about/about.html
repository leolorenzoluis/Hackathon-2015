About

(Search for Help in the Electronic Label Database) lets you find over-the-counter medication that's right for you. Find a drug that fits your problem, without any of the ingredients you want to avoid. Using SHIELD, you can quickly and easily check drug labels to make sure they meet your needs.

SHIELD was created by Pyramid Systems for the GSA's Agile Delivery Services BPA. It was created using free, open-source technologies.

Data Accuracy

SHIELD uses data from the openFDA API based on the FDA's electronic database of drug labels. openFDA is a beta research project and not for clinical use. While we make every effort to ensure that data is accurate, you should assume all results are unvalidated. Neither this site nor the FDA ensure or warrant the efficacy or safety of medication available "over-the-counter".
